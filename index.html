3Common Ticket Scenarios

Know It Before It Hits the Queue

These are the most common support patterns across Harvey's customer base. Click each to see the recommended approach — your enablement content should make these instinctive.

🧑‍🏫
Sam — QA Lead

About 40% of escalations trace back to one of these five patterns. When a specialist knows these cold, escalation rates drop fast. That's exactly why enablement content matters.

"Harvey's answer doesn't cite a source — is it hallucinating?" ▼
This is the #1 anxiety for new users in legal. Clarify: Harvey cites sources when grounding its answer in documents or legal databases via the Knowledge product. For general reasoning, drafting, or summarization tasks, responses are generated — not retrieved. That's expected behavior, not a bug. Guide users toward Knowledge for citation-heavy research workflows.
"I uploaded a document to Vault but I can't find it." ▼
Usually a permissions or workspace context issue. Check whether the user uploaded to a Shared Space vs. their personal workspace. Documents in a shared space may not appear in individual search without the right access level. Walk them through the Vault filter panel — most users haven't found it yet.
"My Harvey Agent stopped mid-task — what happened?" ▼
Agents pause on ambiguous inputs or missing context. Direct the user to the Agent's task log in Command Center — it shows exactly where it stopped and why. This is also a great trigger to share a quick-reference guide on writing clearer agent prompts for their firm's specific workflows.
"How do I know if our team is actually using Harvey?" ▼
Point them to Command Center — built exactly for this. Adoption metrics, usage by feature, and benchmarking against peer firms are all available there. If they're not seeing data, check that their admin has enabled firm-level reporting under their account settings.
"Can Harvey review contracts in languages other than English?" ▼
Yes — Harvey supports multilingual contract review through Contract Intelligence. Performance varies by language and document complexity, so set accurate expectations upfront. Refer them to Harvey Academy for a step-by-step walkthrough on optimizing multilingual workflows.
